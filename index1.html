About Us

NITJ Food Festival 2020 :It’s a feast ,not a fest ....

As a part of our commitment to the all-round development of students and the welfare of society , we at Dr. B R Ambedkar National Institute Of Technology ,Jalandhar , Punjab, India are organizing the second edition of NITJ Food Festival which attracts thousands of students from across the country to participate in a 2 days extravaganza comprising of food competitions like making a Guinness Book Of World Record, Food-Fie Mania, Master-Chef NITJ , Foodie-Fights and various pre-fest workshops. We are expecting a huge crowd as well as enthusiasm from people in and out at Jalandhar and Punjab.

Our Ultimate Aim :The ultimate aim of this Food Festival is setting up of an NGO/Funds generated will be donated to an already running NGO that will work for hunger elimination from India . The two major aims of this NGO will be :
a)Feeding the underprivileged(Proper Utilization of Food) :The wastage of food is really very unhealthy not only for poor but for our country aswell. So the major purpose will be proper utilization of leftover food from parties ,restaurants, hostel mess,etc to someone who is really in need .
b)Setting up of Kitchens: To provide nutritious food to people at a nominal price ,we will be setting up kitchens under this NGO programme that will help us in our mission to have a #HungerFreeIndia.
